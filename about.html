Meet The Director



The Tidewater Concert Band has provided musical entertainment ranging from Sousa Style Marches, Big Band, Swing, Blues, Broadway Musicals, etc. in the Hampton Roads area since 1971. Currently under the direction of Maestro Chris Serrano and Associate Conductor Josiah Wilson, the band is currently made up of volunteer musicians from every walks of life including local school band directors, band students, former and current military musicians... to form a fully-instrumented top-notch professional performing organization.
